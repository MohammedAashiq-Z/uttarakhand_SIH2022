Flavours of Land

Bal-Mithai

Bal-Mithai

✦ Bal mithai originated in the Uttarakhand region. It was an invention of Lala Joga Shah of Lal Bazaar, Almora, in the early twentieth century. Scholars believe that bal mithai must have been offering to the Sun God.

Gulgula

Gulgula

✦ Gulgula is traditional sweet made in different regions of Uttarkhand mainly in Nainital. It is one of the most popular sweets in the market places, it is traditionally made on specific festive occasions in rural areas.

Ras

Ras

✦ Ras which is a thick black color soup made from the mixture of whole pulses available in Uttarankahand in some parts (Pithoragarh) it is called Chudkani.

Famous Places of Land

Naini Lake

Naini Lake

✦Nainital, also known as Naini Lake, is a natural freshwater body, situated amidst the township of Nainital in Kumaon. It is a kidney shaped and has an outfall at the southeastern end. Nainital, along with other lakes of Kumaon, is integral to tourism and recreation in Kumaon

The Mall Road

Mall Road

✦ The Mall is a most popular name in Nainital city. A busiest road in day time, The Mall runs side by side of the Nainital Lake. Britishers constructed The Mall Road which passes through the centre of Nainital and connects two main ends of Nainital city,

Nainital Ropeway

Nainital Ropeway

✦ The Aerial Ropeway in Nainital is one of the biggest tourist attractions. With hundreds of visitors flying in each day, aerial ropeway carries people from Mallital to the Snow View Point, one of the popular places to visit in Nainital.
