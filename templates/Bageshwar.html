Bal mithai

Bal mithai

Bal mithai (Kumaoni: बाल मिठाई) is a brown chocolate-like fudge, made with roasted khoya,
coated with white sugar balls, and is a popular sweet from the Himalayan state of Uttarakhand in India, especially regions around Kumaon.
