Flavours of Land

Dhokla

Dhokla

✦Dhokla is a vegetarian culinary dish
that is native to Pithoragarph.
It is also commonly served in other parts of India.
It is made with a fermented batter derived from legumes like Chickpea, Pigeon pea and Urad and rice.

Khandvi

Khandvi

✦Khandvi,also known as Patuli,
Dahivadi or Suralichi Vadi
, is a savory snack in Maharashtrian cuisine
as well as in Gujarati cuisine of India.

Undhiyu

Undhiyu

✦Undhiyu is a mixed vegetable dish
that is a regional specialty of Pithoragarh.
The name of this dish comes from the Gujarati words "matlu"
meaning earthen pot and "undhu" meaning upside down
since they have been traditionally cooked upside down underground in earthen pots fired from above.

Famous Places of Land

Kafni

Kafni

✦The scenic beauty and the tranquility prevailing in this
region has an unbeatable charm.
The Kaphini (Kafni) Glacier lies left of
the Pindar Valley below the famous peak of Nandakot.

Askot Sanctuary

Askot Sanctuary

✦A heaven for wildlife lovers and botany enthusiast,
Askot sanctuary is adorned with wide range of flora and fauna.
Located at the height of 5412 m and at a distance of 54 km from Pithoragarh,
this place is bounded by rich and gorgeous surroundings.

Munsiari

Munsiari

✦A small and gorgeous town in Himalayas,
Munsiyari is almost 127 km from Pithoragarh.
A paradise of wild flowers, lake and other raw form of nature,
it is also said to be the gateway to Himalayan range's Johar region.
Munsiyari is also the originating place of the River Gori Ganga.
