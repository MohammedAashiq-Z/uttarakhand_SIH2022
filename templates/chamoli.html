Famous Places of Land

Gopeshwar

Gopeshwar township

✦Gopeshwar is famous for its weather and
its very beautiful temple "Gopinath" one can definitely experience the feeling of being there in Gopinath. Chamoli Gopeshwar has a very beautiful kund that is Vaitrani.

Vasudhara Falls

Vasudhara Falls

✦ 'Vasu' refers to Lord Vishnu's Vasudeva. Dhara means "path of the river".
Uttarakhand is surrounded by waterfalls.
Vasudhara waterfall is set in the background of a 145 m cliff. Nearby mountains are Chaukhamba, Nilkantha and Balakun.

Gopinath Temple

Gopinath Mandir

✦ Gopinath Temple is an ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Shiva in Gopeshwar, Chamoli District. It is situated in Gopeshwar village now part of Gopeshwar town. The temple stands out in its architectural proficiency; it is topped by a magnificent dome and the 30 sq ft sanctum sanctorum.
