Famous Places of Land

Sahastradhara

Sahastradhara

✦Sahastradhara, also known as
‘Thousand Fold Spring’,

Tiger Falls

Tiger Falls

✦Tiger Falls, situated in the beautiful forests of Chakrata,
offers pristine views of Mother Nature.
With an elevation of 312 m,
this is one of the highest direct waterfalls in India.

Tapkeshwar Temple

✦Tapkeshwar Temple in Dehradun,

making for an interesting spectacle.
